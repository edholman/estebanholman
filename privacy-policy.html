4. SMS/Text Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number and submitting a form on this website, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Incite LLC. These messages may include:

Consent is not a condition of purchase. Your information collected via SMS opt-in will not be shared with or sold to third parties for marketing purposes.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
